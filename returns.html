Eligibility & Exclusions

Check if your item qualifies

Category Returnable? Conditions
Textiles, Apparel Yes Unworn, unwashed, tags intact, folds intact.
Handicrafts, Blue Pottery Yes Intact with original cushioning/box. No scratches/chips.
Packaged Spices / Groceries Yes Sealed, untampered packaging; within shelf life.
Personal Care (unopened) Yes Factory seal intact; no signs of use.
Personalized / Tailored items No Non-returnable unless damaged/defective on arrival.
Used / Worn items No Hygiene and quality reasons.
Clearance / Final Sale No Marked as non-returnable at purchase.

Approval subject to quality check upon receipt.

How Returns Work

Step-by-step

1
Generate your RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) below or write to us via Contact.
2
Pack the item securely with all accessories/tags and include the printed label.
3
Hand over to the assigned pickup partner or ship to our address (mentioned on the label).
4
QC on arrival (24–48 hours) — we’ll notify you.
5
Refund/credit issued in 2–4 working days after QC.
Refund mode: Original payment method or store credit (your choice in the form).
