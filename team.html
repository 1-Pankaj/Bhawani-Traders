Rajesh Sharma — Founder & MD

Started Bhawani Traders in 1985 at Johari Bazar. Rajesh believes in fair pricing, genuine products, and old-school service with a smile.

Favorite product category: Blue Pottery. Weekend ritual: sourcing trips across Rajasthan.

Anita Verma — Head of Operations

From procurement to delivery SLAs, Anita orchestrates it all. She’s the force behind our reliable, on-time shipments.

Toolbox: dashboards, checklists, and chai.

Imran Khan — Artisan Relations Lead

Works closely with artisan cooperatives ensuring fair trade and quality. Leads design collaborations for exclusive collections.

Clusters: Sanganer, Bagru, Pokhran, Bikaner.

Priya Sharma — Customer Care Lead

Heads the team answering queries, coordinating returns, and turning first-time buyers into regulars.

Vikram Patel — Marketing Manager

Crafts data-driven campaigns and community programs to celebrate Rajasthan’s heritage.

Neha Gupta — Warehouse Supervisor

Leads a passionate fulfilment crew. Motto: “Right product, right box, right time.”
