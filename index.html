About Bhawani Traders

Your trusted partner since 1985

Founded in 1985 in the heart of Jaipur, Bhawani Traders has been serving the people of Rajasthan with quality products and exceptional service for over three decades. What started as a small neighborhood store has grown into one of the most trusted names in retail across the state.

At Bhawani Traders, we take pride in offering a unique blend of traditional Rajasthani products and modern daily essentials. Our carefully curated selection includes authentic handicrafts, textiles, spices, and more, alongside a comprehensive range of groceries and household items.

  • 100% authentic products
  • Direct from artisans
  • Fair trade practices
  • Across Rajasthan
  • Secure payments
  • Easy returns
