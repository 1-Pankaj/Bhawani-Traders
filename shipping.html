Packaging & Handling

Designed to protect and reduce waste

  • Shock-absorbent cushioning for fragile items (blue pottery, glass, etc.).
  • Moisture-resistant liners for spices and textiles.
  • Tamper-evident seals on parcels.
  • Where possible, we use recycled/recyclable materials.

If your item arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos. See Returns for steps.

Coverage & Exceptions

Remote areas may need extra time

We deliver to most districts across Rajasthan. Certain remote or restricted areas may have:

  • Longer delivery timeframes (add 1–2 working days).
  • COD unavailability or higher minimum order value.
  • Occasional address verification by the courier.

You’ll see any such constraints at checkout based on your pincode.
